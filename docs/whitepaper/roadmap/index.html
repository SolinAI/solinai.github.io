📅 Roadmap

Phase 1: Proof of Concept and Infrastructure Development​

Expected Completion: Q4 2024
Goals:

  1. Design the technical framework for the Solin project and define the core features of the decentralized social platform.
  2. Initiate infrastructure development, including blockchain selection, smart contract development, and decentralized storage solutions.
  3. Develop a decentralized identity management system (based on Soulbound Tokens, SBT) to provide users with unique identity markers and a social credit system.
  4. Begin releasing the white paper, conducting project marketing, and warming up the community.

Key Tasks:

  1. Select and deploy a blockchain platform (e.g., Base/Solana).
  2. Design and deploy smart contracts.
  3. Integrate decentralized storage solutions (e.g., IPFS/Arweave).
  4. Complete and launch the white paper and project website.
  5. Start building an early community and attract creators and early adopters.

Phase 2: Platform Launch and Initial Promotion​

Expected Completion: Q1 2025
Goals:

  1. Officially launch the Solin social platform with comprehensive social features, entering the public beta stage.
  2. Implement decentralized user identity management and a social credit system based on SBT.
  3. Begin circulating platform tokens and promoting the ecosystem to attract content creators, key opinion leaders (KOLs), and general users.

Key Tasks:

  1. Launch the platform with core social features such as content posting, commenting, liking, and sharing.
  2. Activate the decentralized identity system (SBT) and generate unique social credit profiles for each user.
  3. Implement a token incentive mechanism where users earn tokens through content creation and social interactions.
  4. Execute marketing campaigns and collaborate with KOLs to increase platform visibility.
  5. Conduct token sales and market promotion to attract more users and investors.

Phase 3: Ecosystem Expansion and User Growth​

Expected Completion: Q3 2025
Goals:

  1. Introduce more features, such as a KOL governance mechanism and cross-chain interoperability, to enhance platform flexibility and scalability.
  2. Organize platform activities to promote community development and attract more creators and general users.
  3. Expand the user base and achieve initial ecosystem stability.

Key Tasks:

  1. Launch a KOL governance mechanism, enabling KOLs to participate in platform decision-making and influence its direction.
  2. Enable cross-chain compatibility, allowing seamless interactions across different blockchain networks.
  3. Expand social interaction features, such as a social task system and creator-exclusive functions.
  4. Initiate a global user growth plan through online and offline activities and partnerships to promote platform internationalization.

Phase 4: Comprehensive Optimization and Global Expansion​

Expected Completion: Q1 2026
Goals:

  1. Further optimize the platform’s user experience and introduce more blockchain-based innovative features, ensuring a leading position in the Web3 social domain.
  2. Expand the platform’s global user base to achieve worldwide coverage, with a target of building a community of 10 million active users.
  3. Promote creator tokenization and the flow of value within and beyond the platform, establishing a robust social economy ecosystem.

Key Tasks:

  1. Optimize platform performance, improve user experience, and reduce transaction costs and latency.
  2. Continuously refine content creation and interaction reward mechanisms to maintain high levels of engagement from creators and users.
  3. Complete platform internationalization, supporting multilingual and cross-cultural content distribution.
  4. Facilitate creator tokenization by helping top content creators issue personal tokens (IP tokens) to monetize their personal brands.
  5. Achieve global user targets and sustain growth in social interactions, creator revenues, and community engagement.

Phase 5: Platform Maturity and Long-Term Development​

Expected Completion: Q2 2026 and Beyond
Goals:

  1. Establish Solin as a global leading decentralized Web3 social platform with robust content creation, community governance, and asset management features.
  2. Become the primary social platform for content creators, brands, KOLs, and users, fostering ongoing ecosystem prosperity.

Key Tasks:

  1. Deepen creator tokenization, building a unique brand economy to promote two-way value flows between creators and users.
  2. Continuously improve the decentralized governance system to support a more transparent community decision-making process.
